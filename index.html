PokeMAN Go

First-place winner of the inaugural YVR Comedy Hackathon. Over the course of of two days, our team developed a parody game of the popular 'Pokemon Go'. Our app allowed users to 'catch' different categories of people through the use of facial recognition technology. We built our project using Ruby on Rails, HTML, W3-CSS, Javascript, PostgreSQL, and the FacePlusPlus API. We also relied heavily on proper Git practices and version control.
